As an administrator, I want to be able to generate reports on student performance and attendance, so that I can identify areas for improvement and make data-driven decisions.  Acceptance Criteria:  The system must allow administrators to generate reports on student performance and attendance. The reports must be accurate and up-to-date. The reports must be easy to understand and present data in a clear manner. The system must allow administrators to filter and customize reports based on specific criteria, such as grade level, subject, or time period.
